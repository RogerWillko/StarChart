How to Use Star Chart

🌠 Dashboard

Tap on any hour to give a star.
Green = Star earned
Red = Star lost due to behavior

🧹 Chore List

Add chores that can be done to earn back lost stars.
Mark them complete when your child finishes the task.

🏆 Prizes

Credits are automatically awarded when your child reaches the daily star goal.
Use those credits to redeem fun rewards.

Tips:

• The app saves everything automatically in your browser.
• Works offline once loaded.
• Best used on a tablet or phone the child can easily tap.
